Product Lifecycle Tracker

Track the journey of eco-friendly products — from creation to recycling — and learn how sustainable materials reduce waste.

Lifecycle Stages

Manufacturing

Products begin their journey in factories. Using recycled or renewable materials reduces emissions from the start.

Distribution

Efficient logistics and local sourcing help cut carbon emissions from long-distance transport.

Usage

Consumers play a big role — extending product life through maintenance, repair, or reuse minimizes waste.

Recycling

At the end of its life, responsible disposal and recycling return materials to the production cycle, closing the loop.
